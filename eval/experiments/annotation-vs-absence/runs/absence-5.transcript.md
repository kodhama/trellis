**One consequential design choice — AND, not OR.** The brief says "filter by one or more tags" but doesn't pin the combining semantics. I chose **AND** (each added tag *narrows* results — standard faceted-filter behavior) over OR (each added tag *widens*). This is the load-bearing decision and it's genuinely a judgment call — flip it to OR by changing the subset test `required <= item_tags` to an intersection check, and one test's expected IDs. **I'm flagging it for you to ratify rather than treating it as settled.** I also made tag matching case-insensitive for parity with the title search; say if you'd rather tags be exact.
